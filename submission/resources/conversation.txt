I spoke with a gym trainer named Alex Kazongo who operates as a personal fitness coach in Nairobi. He offers both physical and online training sessions for clients looking to improve their fitness and overall health.

He provides a variety of services including personal training, weight loss programs, muscle building, and online coaching. His target audience includes both men and women, especially young adults who want to build strength, lose weight, and maintain a healthy lifestyle.

He prefers a strong, modern, and masculine style for his website, with bold and attractive colors such as deep navy, black, and teal accents. He wanted the website to feel powerful, motivating, and professional, reflecting discipline and results.

The most important information to display on the website includes the services offered, images of training sessions or results, and clear contact details for potential clients.

The main goal of the website is to allow clients to easily understand his services and quickly reach out through phone, email, or WhatsApp to start their fitness journey.
